Professional Maintenance

Making sure everything runs smoothly and safely

We understand the significance of maintaining a safe, healthy, and attractive environment, and we’re committed to ensuring that your community always looks its best. We’re dedicated to promoting order and following rules to ensure the well-being of everyone in the community. With our expertise in maintenance and repairs, you can trust that your community is in good hands with us.

Common Area Maintenance

Total community care with expert services and support

At our company, our main goal is to ensure that your community is always well-maintained. We take pride in keeping everything running smoothly and in good condition, so you can feel at ease and enjoy the beauty of your surroundings.

What we provide:

  • Maintaining and improving building drainage
  • Managing and executing capital projects
  • Crafting and repairing wooden structures
  • Restoring and renovating common areas
  • Clearing out gutters and preventing blockages
  • Assisting with insurance claims and processes
  • Keeping the building clean and orderly through janitorial and porter work
  • Providing lighting and electrical solutions
  • Painting surfaces to restore and refresh appearance
  • Troubleshooting and repairing plumbing issues
  • Conducting proactive maintenance to avoid future problems
  • Patching and fixing damaged sheetrock
  • Offering emergency services 24/7, all year long

Home Improvement & Repairs

Making sure everything is done to your satisfaction

Our team of experts is here to take care of all your home and community maintenance needs. No job is too big or too small for us to handle! We’re committed to providing efficient, high-quality service that you can rely on.

What we provide:

  • We offer carpentry services to help you with your woodworking needs
  • Count on us for emergency assistance when you need it most
  • Our handyman services can tackle all sorts of odd jobs and repairs
  • Lighting and electrical services to brighten up your space and keep it safe
  • Painting services to give your walls and surfaces a fresh look
  • Plumbing services to fix leaks, clogs, and other issues
  • Sheetrock repairs to patch up any holes or damage in your walls
  • And there’s even more we can do to help! Just let us know what you need, and we’ll do our best to make it happen.

Emergency Services, Resources & Restoration

We’re here for your community during difficult times

When the unexpected strikes, our local response teams are trained to provide proper disaster relief, assess damages, and restore your property. You can rely on us to have your back and take charge and get things back to normal as quickly as possible.

Emergency Response Services:

  • Earthquake damage recovery and repair.
  • Our team is equipped to handle corrosion control and repair to prevent further damage.
  • We provide comprehensive services for fire and smoke damage restoration.
  • Hail damage can be devastating, but we have the expertise to restore your property.
  • We offer assistance with insurance claim processing to help you navigate the process smoothly.
  • Mold can be a health hazard, but our mold removal and remediation services will ensure a safe and clean environment.
  • Our team can help you recover from water damage due to floods and severe weather.
  • Water mitigation, extraction, and repair services are available to help minimize the damage and restore your property.

Restoration and Construction services:

  • We offer a range of construction services to improve your community and clubhouse, including additions and renovations.
  • Our team can handle complete community rebuilds to revitalize your neighborhood.
  • If you’re dealing with construction defects, we provide remediation services to correct the problem.
  • Exterior renovations can give your property a fresh new look and boost curb appeal.
  • We specialize in interior renovations to modernize your living spaces and common areas.
  • Our professional project management team will oversee your project from start to finish, ensuring it is completed on time, within budget, and to your satisfaction.
