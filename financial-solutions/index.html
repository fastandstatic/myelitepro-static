Financial Solutions

Simplify your HOA loan experience with our assistance

Our team is dedicated to providing comprehensive assistance throughout the entire HOA loan process.

What we provide:

  • We provide a comprehensive range of services to help you secure HOA loans with ease, including reviewing your loan request to determine your funding needs.
  • Our team will prepare a thorough financial analysis to confirm your funding requirements and ensure you have the necessary resources.
  • We communicate directly with contractors to ensure they have the information they need to provide accurate estimates.
  • Our team can request and collect legal review of governing documents to ensure compliance with all applicable regulations.
  • We complete loan application packages and submit them to the appropriate lenders on your behalf.
  • Our team coordinates with your community manager to ensure a smooth and efficient process.
  • We respond to lender inquiries promptly and professionally, providing document updates as needed.
  • Our team coordinates the final signature between the board, attorney, and lender to ensure a successful closing.
  • We can facilitate wire transfer requests quickly and securely to ensure timely disbursement of funds.
